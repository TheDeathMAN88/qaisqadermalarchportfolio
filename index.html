About Me

Passionate Architecture Student

I'm Qais Qadermal, a high school student with a deep passion for architecture and design. My journey in architecture began with a fascination for how spaces influence human emotions and behaviors. I believe that great architecture is not just about buildings, but about creating experiences that resonate with people.

Through my studies and projects, I've developed skills in 3D modeling, technical drafting, and architectural visualization. I'm constantly exploring new ways to integrate sustainability, technology, and aesthetics in my designs.
